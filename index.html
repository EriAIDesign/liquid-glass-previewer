What is Liquid Glass?

Liquid Glass is the semi-transparent, blurred effect Apple uses in macOS Dock and iOS customization mode. This tool simulates the real appearance with accurate blur, vibrancy, and light reflection.

How to use

Upload your app icon (512×512px recommended), then switch background colors to see how your icon adapts. The Liquid Glass preview shows the effect as it appears on real devices.

Privacy & Safety

Your images are processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Your files never leave your device.
